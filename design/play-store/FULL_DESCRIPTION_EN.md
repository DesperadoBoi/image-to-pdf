# Full description — EN

## Google Play copy

ImageToPDF turns existing images and camera captures into a single document without adding
a complicated workflow. Choose your pages, review them, and save the result where you want
it.

CORE FEATURES

• Import multiple images and keep them in the order you select.
• Browse recent photos and albums in the built-in gallery.
• Add pages with the camera, Android photo picker, or system file browser.
• Review every page before creating the document.

DOCUMENT SCANNING

Capture several pages in one scanning session. After each shot, adjust the four document
corners yourself, rotate or retake the page, or keep the original image. When the scan is
ready, its pages move into the same editor used for imported images.

PAGE EDITING

Drag pages into a new order, remove unwanted ones, and rotate images. The full-screen editor
supports zooming and manual rectangular cropping. Perspective correction is available in
the scanning flow and is controlled manually.

EXPORT

Choose a file name and one of three quality levels. Optional settings cover image-sized or
A4 pages, automatic, portrait, or landscape orientation, and page margins. Android's system
dialog lets you choose where to save the file. Once it is ready, open it or share it through
another installed app.

PRIVACY

Images and documents are processed locally on your device. The app does not upload them to
the developer's server and does not need internet access to create the result.

NO ADS OR ACCOUNTS

ImageToPDF has no advertising, analytics, accounts, or registration requirement.

CURRENT VERSION LIMITS

The app does not include text recognition, automatic document edge detection, or automatic
capture. It also does not provide cloud storage, editing of existing PDFs, electronic
signatures, password protection, or PDF compression.
